Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help improve your browsing experience by remembering preferences and providing personalized content.

  • Improve overall user experience

We may allow third-party services like Google AdSense to place cookies on your device. These cookies help deliver personalized ads based on your browsing activity.

You can learn more and opt out of personalized ads here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies or receive a warning before they are stored.
